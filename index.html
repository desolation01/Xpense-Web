Core experience

Everything is shaped around a monthly spending story.

The page follows the same flow as the product: understand the month, inspect the week, then zoom into the day when you need detail.

01

One glance tells you where the month stands.

Salary, remaining balance, weekly pace, and current month totals stay visible so you can make faster decisions without opening separate panels.

02

Calendar cells become your fastest input surface.

Click any date, add an expense or gain, and keep a traceable record tied to the actual day it happened. The flow favors speed and memory over rigid forms.

03

Insights stay practical instead of abstract.

Category splits, monthly summaries, and AI prompts help you see patterns, compare momentum, and adjust before the next week drifts off course.
